Data Analyst & Engineer

Personal Details

I love crunching data and coming up with Unique and insightful dashboards .

I always thought I would become a great mechanical engineer because of my deep understanding of the sciences and mathematics, hence why I initially pursued a degree in Mechanical engineering in University.

For the most part, it was an enjoyable experience but once I reached the final year and had to conduct my dissertation on the loading history in turbine blades, I had to adapt and develop new sets of skills that involved gathering large data from computer simulations. This was my doorway into data analytics— where my love for the subject began.

Since then I've relocated from the UK to the USA, explored data analytics more by attending the intensive SMU 6-month Course on Data Science, and put these skills to practice in-class projects.
